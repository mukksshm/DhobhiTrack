# DhobiTrack 🧺

## 📌 Project Overview
DhobiTrack is a Smart Laundry Tracking System designed to simplify laundry management for college students. In many colleges, students often face confusion about the status of their clothes—whether they are waiting to be washed, currently being processed, or ready for pickup.
This application solves that problem by providing a simple and user-friendly platform where users can check the real-time status of their laundry anytime, without the need to physically visit or repeatedly ask the laundry staff.

## 🚀 Key Features
* User-friendly interface for easy navigation
* Track laundry status in real-time
* Multiple stages tracking:
  * Submitted
  * Washing
  * Ready for Pickup
* Reduces confusion and unnecessary trips
* Improves transparency in laundry management

## 🎯 Objective
The main objective of DhobiTrack is to bring transparency and efficiency to college laundry systems by allowing students to track their laundry status digitally and in real-time.
